Who are we...

Blenheim Greenpower is a co-curricular club at Blenheim High School, Epsom for years 7 - 11. In accordance with Greenpower Education Trust's ethos our aim is to inspire Blenheim students to excel in Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths (STEM) through the unique challenge of designing , building and racing a Formula 24 electric car. A basic car kit is purchased and it is up to teams to modify the bodywork and controls. Blenheim students compete in events in organised by Greenpower Education Trust at iconic tracks such as Goodwood Motor Circuit and Dunsfold Park. Unlike traditional motorsport events speed and finishing first isn't how you win. Driving efficiently and completing the most laps using the power of two 12V rechargeable batteries is how you win.

Why is the club beneficial...

This section explains how taking part in the Blenheim Greenpower club can help benefit you.

  • Communication

    Whether it's racing or building the cars, communication is key to get the best end result.

  • Technology

    In the club you will learn how to do basic wiring, use new tools and problem solve with technology.

  • Coding

    This has become a large part of the club whether you're programming the website or the car this is all a key process.

  • Teamwork

    Arguably the most important as working as a team is the only way that we will be able to sucseed.

  • Friends

    Along the way you will meet coutless of new friends and also form new groups of friends with similiar interests.

  • Events

    In the club you will be invited to many differents events from races to testing the car.
